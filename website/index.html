What it does

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Pitch count tracking

Track every pitch in real time with a single tap. Configurable limits and rest-day thresholds alert you before a pitcher hits mandatory rest requirements.

Catcher innings

Automatically tracks innings behind the plate and flags when a catcher approaches limits. Pitcher-catcher eligibility conflicts are highlighted immediately.

Game history

Every game is saved automatically. Review past pitcher counts, rest requirements, and scores at a glance — even when offline.

League configurations

Create named configurations with custom pitch limits, rest thresholds, and catcher rules. Switch between league settings when starting a new game.

Game summaries

Generate and email a formatted game summary with pitcher data, umpire notes, and final scores — ready to send to your league administrator.

Fully offline

No account, no internet required. All data stays on your device. Works in the dugout even without a signal.

How it works

Up and running before first pitch.

1
Configure your league

Set pitch limits, rest thresholds, and catcher inning rules once — save them as a named configuration to reuse every game.

2
Start a game

Enter team names, field, date, and your opening pitcher and catcher for each team. Takes under a minute.

3
Tap to track

Hit + Pitch for each throw, Next Batter when the at-bat ends, End Half Inning when sides change. Undo if you miscount.

4
Send the report

After the game, generate the summary and email it directly from the app to your player agent or league office.
